### Snake World Models (2025)
Implementing EqM-based world models that learn energy landscapes over state-action spaces. Instead of fixed-horizon integration like diffusion models, we use gradient descent to find optimal next states and actions simultaneously.

**Key Innovation**: Joint optimization of state and action in a learned energy landscape conditioned on current state, enabling adaptive compute and natural planning behavior.

**Applications**: 
- Sequential decision making (Snake, games)
- ARC-AGI challenges
- Robotics and embodied tasks
